Integrations connect your support channels to FrontDesk, allowing you to manage all customer conversations from a single unified inbox. When customers reach out through connected channels, their messages automatically sync to FrontDesk in real-time.

## Setting up integrations

To configure integrations for your organization:

1. Go to Organization Settings â†’ Integrations
2. Select the integration you want to set up
3. Follow the setup instructions for that integration

## Available integrations

### Support channels

- **[Discord](/integrations/discord)**: Connect your Discord server to FrontDesk. When users create threads in your support channels, they automatically sync to FrontDesk.
- **[Slack](/integrations/slack)**: Connect your Slack workspace to FrontDesk. When users create threads in your support channels, they automatically sync to FrontDesk.
