# A Fresh Thread Toolbar

Your most common support actions now live in one place. The redesigned thread toolbar brings Reply, Support Intelligence, and quick actions together into a single, streamlined interface at the bottom of every thread.

![New thread toolbar showing Reply, Support Intelligence, Resolve, and Next buttons](https://public-files.tryfrontdesk.app/changelogs/2026-04-20/thread-toolbar.png)
{/* Screenshot should show: the new bottom toolbar with Reply button, Support Intelligence button with sparkles icon, Resolve button, and Next button */}

The toolbar stays clean and compact when you're scanning conversations, then expands smoothly when you need to act. Click Reply to compose your response, or open Support Intelligence to get AI-powered help, all without losing sight of the thread.

## Quick Actions Panel

Support Intelligence suggestions now surface directly in the toolbar. When AI detects a relevant status change, label, or duplicate thread, you'll see the recommendation right where you're working, no hunting through sidebars or extra clicks.

![Quick Actions panel showing AI suggestions for labels and status](https://public-files.tryfrontdesk.app/changelogs/2026-04-20/quick-actions.png)
{/* Screenshot should show: the expanded quick actions panel with a lightning bolt icon, suggested labels, and status change recommendation with accept/dismiss buttons */}

Review suggestions with full context, including the AI's reasoning, then accept or dismiss with a single click. Accept all recommendations at once when they're spot-on, or handle them individually when you need more control.

## Keyboard-First Workflow

Navigate your inbox without touching your mouse. The new toolbar comes with dedicated keyboard shortcuts designed for speed:

- **R** — Start a new reply
- **B** — Open Support Intelligence chat
- **⌘⌥R** — Resolve the thread
- **J** — Jump to the next thread

Chain these shortcuts together to power through your queue: resolve a thread, jump to the next, and start replying in seconds.

## Smoother Animations

Every panel transition now animates gracefully. The toolbar expands and contracts with fluid motion, and switching between Reply and Support Intelligence feels natural rather than jarring. Small details like these add up to a more polished, responsive experience.

# Other Improvements

- **Redesigned Thread Header**: The first message in a thread now displays with improved spacing and a cleaner author layout, making it easier to scan the opening context of a conversation.
- **Unified Panel Architecture**: Reply composition and Support Intelligence chat share a single expandable container, reducing visual clutter and keeping focus on the conversation.
- **Contextual Resolve Button**: The Resolve button smartly hides when a thread is already resolved, decluttering the toolbar when that action isn't relevant.
- **Tool Call Visibility**: When Support Intelligence searches your documentation or drafts a reply, you'll see exactly what it's doing with detailed tool call displays including search queries and results.
